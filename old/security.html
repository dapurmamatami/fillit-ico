Safety first

How safe is my money with Fillit?

Your money is stored in a certified ring-fenced bank account with FCA licensed e-money institution in Gibraltar. We treat security of our member very seriously, and do everything and a ton more to prevent customers' funds from being compromised.

How can I make my account more secure?

Here are few tips how to make your Fillit account more secure:
Always use a strong password for your account
Remember your password by heart, or store it in a special password management utility such as KeePass.
Do not save your password in your browser.
Avoid accessing Loadoo from unsafe locations such as internet cafes, through untrusted WiFi's and just with people standing behind you.
Always have latest antivirus and firewall software installed to prevent viruses from infiltrating your PC and stealing your login information.
We STRONGLY engourage to use our Second-Factor Authentication system (2FA). This makes account access impossible even if someone would hijack your login details.
Never share your card details with anyone. If you believe your card details were exposed to a third person, or stolen by a malicious website - please contact us immediately for an assistance.

I received an email from Fillit asking for my PIN and password. Is this email real?

Fillit will never ask you for your login details, card details (except last 4 digits of the card), or any of your personal information. If you receive an email asking you for any of such information - this is a scam attempt, and you should report it to us immediately.

What is two-factor authentication (2FA)?

2FA (Second-Factor Authentication) is a method of making your account almost impossible to hack. It works the following way:
  1. Install Google Authenticator or Authy app on your smartphone. Both are available for both Android and IOS devices.
  2. Open your 2FA app.
  3. Go to your Fillit account, navigate to "Security"
  4. A huge QR code will appear on the screen ("")
  5. Please scan it with your 2FA app on your phone.
  6. A security code will appear in your app.
  7. Enter it below the QR code and click "Verify".
  8. If everything is correct, your phone will be connected to your Fillit account. During next login to your Fillit account, you will be asked for a six-digit code, that will appear in your 2FA app.
  9. Security of your Fillit account is now much more improved!

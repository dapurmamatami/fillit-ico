What is Fillit Debit debit card?

Fillit debit card is a real visa card that can be funded with any currency. Thus make it possible to make any payment in the real world by using it at any shop or restaurant and online shopping. Furthermore, it can be used by companies to pay staff or suppliers.

Which currencies does Fillit card support?

Fillit card can be issued in Euros.

What are the delivery terms for the Fillit Debit Card?

Fillit card can be delivered via two different methods:
  1. Standard untracked delivery
    Free of charge, takes from 7 to 30 days depending on shipping address.
  2. Express tracked delivery
    Please check our fees table for the cost. Card will be delivered within 4-7 business days.

Can I order more than one Loadoo Debit Card?

Fillit allows 1 virtual and 1 plastic card per customer.

What happens if I lose my card or it gets stolen?

  1. Contact us immediately, so we can block the card to prevent further unauthorized usage.
  2. Please send us a list of transactions you believe were fraudulent.
  3. We will start the investigation and will update you once it's finished.
  4. If card is virtual - fresh card number will be available in your Fillit account for free.
  6. If card is plastic - new card will be issued at an additional fee of 8 EUR.

Can I change my debit card PIN?

Once you have activated your Fillit Debit Card and retrieved the PIN, you can change this PIN at an ATM.
However, not all ATMs offer this feature so the ability to change your Fillit Debit Card PIN will depend on the ATM.

Where is the Fillit Debit Card accepted?

Fillit card is accepted anywhere where VISA cards are allowed.

A few insights on verification of you Fillit account.

What documents should I submit to verify my account?

We require two documents to be provided to verify your Fillit account.
  1. Photo ID
    Should be valid in Europe. Photo should be as bright, sharp and clear as possible, without any modifications such as cropping, resizing or any other corrections, preferably copied directly from your smartphone's camera. We accept the following documents:
  2. ID card
    We require both sides to be submitted as separate images.
  3. Passport
    Full page photo is required.
  4. Driver's license
    We require both sides to be submitted as separate images.
  5. Proof of address
    Document should be as bright, clean and sharp as possible and should be issued earlier than 90 days ago. We prefer PDF versions of documents. The following documents are accepted:
  6. Utility bill
    Water, energy, gas and other utility services.
  7. Bank statement
    It should have your full name and address.
  8. Tax return
    Should contain your name and address.
  9. Council Tax bill
    Should contain your name and address.
  10. Certificate of Residency
    A government-issued document confirming your residential address.

I received a message saying that my information is currently being reviewed. Could you tell me more?

This message means that your documents were received and your account is under review. Once it finished, we will inform you with an outcome via separate email.
Normally the process takes 5-12 business days.

How long does verification take?

In most cases, review takes around 5-12 business days.

Why do I need to verify my account?

As an authorized e-money provider, in order to comply with EU and UK AML legislation, Fillit is required to verify the identity of its customers once certain criterias are met, such as total card annual turnover, ATM limits or requirement to fund the card from third-party merchants such as PayPal.

How to pass Fillit verification?

Just provide the 2 documents that are required by us, and you will be verified in no time.
